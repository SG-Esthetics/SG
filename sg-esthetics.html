04 — PMU

PMU
Services

Prices do not include GST

$350

Microblading

A semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing technique that uses a manual, handheld tool with tiny needles to create realistic, hair-like strokes in the eyebrow area. This process deposits pigment into the upper layer of the skin to simulate natural eyebrow hairs, resulting in a fuller, more defined, and low-maintenance brow shape.

Follow-up appointment required 4–6 weeks after first appointment — $125

$365

Powder Brows

A semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing technique that creates a soft, shaded, and filled-in look, much like the effect of using eyebrow powder or a pencil. This method uses a specialized machine to deposit tiny, pixelated dots of pigment into the upper layers of the skin, resulting in a natural-looking, defined eyebrow shape.

Follow-up appointment required 4–6 weeks after first appointment — $125

$400

Combo Brows

AKA hybrid brows — blends natural hair strokes at the front with powder shading throughout the middle & tail, for a fuller & more defined, yet realistic look. Combines the best of both techniques for enhanced depth and coverage, suitable for those needing more density than Microblading alone offers.

Follow-up appointment required 4–6 weeks after first appointment — $125

$200

Refresher

PMU refresher appointments are maintenance sessions necessary to restore the colour, intensity, definition, and shape of cosmetic tattoos that have naturally faded over time. These appointments are typically recommended every 2–3 years for existing clients.

Timing varies based on lifestyle, skin type, PMU type and desired look
